Mitochondria is the Powerhouse of the Cell" is an expression describing the function of mitochondria organelles found in the cells of eukaryotic organisms. On Tumblr, the phrase is typically mocked as an example of impractical information taught in public schools.

The phrase

"powerhouse of the cell"
